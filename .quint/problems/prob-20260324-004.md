# LLM retry has no backoff and swallows rate limits silently

## Signal

LLMClient.chat() retries 2x on RateLimitError but with no delay/backoff — immediate retry into a rate limit is wasteful and will likely fail again. Also no logging of retries, making debugging blind. Under budget pressure (~1000 calls), wasted retries matter.

## Constraints

- Must stay within API call budget
- No external retry libraries — plain asyncio

## Optimization Targets

- Retry success rate
- Budget efficiency

## Acceptance

Exponential backoff on rate limits. Retry attempts logged with attempt number. Budget tracking includes LLM calls, not just tool calls.

## Blast Radius

llm.py

## Reversibility

high
